Technical FAQ

What is the base of RealmOS?

RealmOS Universe Edition is built on a high-performance MX Linux base (Debian-stable), ensuring rock-solid stability.

Is the E7450 mandatory?

While specifically tuned for the Latitude E7450, it is fully compatible with most 64-bit Intel and AMD systems.
