Distribution FAQ

What is the upstream base?

RealmOS is built upon a high-performance MX Linux foundation (Debian-stable), prioritized for long-term stability.

Hardware Requirements?

Requires a 64-bit processor. While reference-tuned on the E7450, it supports most modern PC architectures.

How to contribute?

Submit your custom Realm modules via Discord. Verified community builds earn a place in the official repository.

Roadmap?

V1.2 is currently in development, focusing on hardware-accelerated rendering and gaming performance.
